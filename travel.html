Azerbaijan Travel Guide

Discover the Land of Fire's Hidden Gems

Must-See Destinations

Baku

Capital

The cosmopolitan capital where medieval Old City meets futuristic architecture like the Flame Towers.

East Azerbaijan

Gobustan

UNESCO

Famous for its ancient rock carvings and mysterious mud volcanoes that bubble and erupt.

60km from Baku

Sheki

Historic

A charming mountain town known for its Khan's Palace with exquisite stained glass work.

Northwest Azerbaijan

Travel Tips

Currency

Azerbaijani Manat (AZN). ATMs widely available in cities.

Language

Azerbaijani (Turkic language) but English spoken in tourist areas.

Best Time

April-June & September-October for pleasant weather.

Visa

eVisa available online for most nationalities.

Suggested Itineraries

Baku Express (3 Days)

Short Trip

Perfect introduction to Azerbaijan's capital and nearby attractions.

  • Day 1: Old City, Maiden Tower, Carpet Museum
  • Day 2: Gobustan Petroglyphs & Mud Volcanoes
  • Day 3: Flame Towers, Heydar Aliyev Center

Cultural Tour (7 Days)

Cultural

Explore Azerbaijan's rich heritage from Baku to the Caucasus Mountains.

  • Days 1-3: Baku highlights
  • Day 4: Shamakhi & Lahij village
  • Days 5-7: Sheki with Khan's Palace
