Azerbaijani Culture

Where Tradition Meets Modernity

Mugham Music

Azerbaijani Mugham is a traditional musical genre that combines classical poetry with musical improvisation. Recognized by UNESCO as a Masterpiece of Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity.

Carpet Weaving

Azerbaijani carpets are renowned worldwide for their intricate designs and craftsmanship. The art of Azerbaijani carpet weaving was inscribed on UNESCO's Representative List of Intangible Cultural Heritage.

Novruz Bayram

The ancient holiday celebrating the arrival of spring and renewal of nature. Recognized by UNESCO, Novruz is celebrated with traditional rituals, festive meals, and public events throughout Azerbaijan.

Ashiq Art

The traditional art of Azerbaijani troubadours who combine poetry, storytelling, dance and musical skills. Ashiq art was inscribed on UNESCO's Representative List of Intangible Cultural Heritage.
